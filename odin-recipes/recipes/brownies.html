Fudge Brownies

Description:

The BEST Chewy Brownies are just as chewy as the boxed brownies but packed with way more chocolate flavor. One bowl recipe made in less than 1 hour!

Ingredients:

  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs and a yolk
  • tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • tsp salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees and grease a 8x8 inch pan
  2. Melt the butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla
  3. Stir in oil and cocoa powder
  4. Fold in flour, baking soda, and salt mixture
  5. Stir in chocolate chips. Then spread into lined baking pan
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until brownies are set. DO NOT OVERCOOK!
  7. Once cooled, serve with ice cream and enjoy!
