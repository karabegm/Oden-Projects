French Toast

Description:

This french toast is delicious and only takes 15 minutes to make! Its recommended that you use quality bread thicker than average sandwich bread but it's not required.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • tsp Vanilla
  • tsp Cinnamon
  • Your choice of bread!

Directions:

  1. Mix together the eggs and milk
  2. add in the sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon
  3. preheat a non-stick pan with oil
  4. Dip both sides of the bread in the mixture and place it in the pan
  5. flip over as necessary till both sides are toasted
  6. serve and enjoy!
