Banana Bread

Description:

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. Why compromise the banana flavor? Friends and family love my recipe and say it's by far the best! It tastes wonderful toasted. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2-3 Mashed Overripe Bananas!!!

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and lightly crease a loaf pan
  2. Combine eggs, salt, butter, and brown sugar
  3. Add in baking soda, mashed bananas, and flour
  4. Pour batter into greased loaf pan
  5. Bake in the oven for 60 minutes and let cool for 10.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
